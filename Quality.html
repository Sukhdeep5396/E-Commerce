Quality

Comfort

Since 2021, ball fiber and feather blend fill is used on all toss pillows and new stationary series, improving durability and resilience when compared with regular polyester fiber fill.

The feather blends we use in our back and seat cushions are sterilized through triple-washing, making them certified hypoallergenic.

Seating suspension systems are designed to distribute weight evenly and work in conjunction with high density, high resiliency foam cushioning for maximum comfort without compromising support.

Specific to each style, cushions are made with the ideal combination of CertiPUR-US® foam and other materials, creating an enhanced seating experience.

Mixed wood frames have the ideal balance of rigidity and flexibility, providing ultimate support with a 10-year warranty.

Curated Covers

A complete cover assortment is curated to offer a range of color, grade, texture, and pattern to suit each unique customer.

All our leather is top grain, aniline dyed, and ethically sourced.

Staffed by a team of experts with extensive tannery experience, our in-house leather lab tests for light fastness, strength, color, and thickness ensuring quality and longevity.

We offer a selection of Performance fabrics which repel liquids, are easy to clean, and are sure to endure whatever life may throw their way.

People

We value the artistry of our craft and are committed to investing in continuous training and an expansive certification process for our staff.

The average tenure for our employees is over 10 years, with some having built careers spanning over 40 years.

Ingenuity often extends from diversity, and with 60 cultures and 40 languages represented among our employees, we are proud of our culture.

Efficiency

Integrating trained specialists and artificial intelligence, we optimize yields from leather hides and sheet stock, reducing cost and waste, leading to a more sustainable practice.

Streamlining our supply chain and optimizing inventory levels helps us reduce lead times, improve our flexibility, and overall efficiency, ensuring you receive your product as quickly as possible.
